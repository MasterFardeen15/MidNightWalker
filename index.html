Smart Lighting Through Motion Detection

Project Overview

Our project addresses the crucial need for energy conservation in modern lighting systems. In a world where energy efficiency is paramount, we noticed that traditional lighting systems often waste energy by remaining active in empty spaces. By developing an AI-powered motion detection system that intelligently controls lighting based on human presence, we're working to create a more sustainable and energy-efficient future.

The key features of our solution include:

  • Real-time human detection using TensorFlow.js
  • Responsive lighting control based on position
  • Energy-efficient operation that automatically dims lights in unused areas
  • Simple and intuitive interface for easy monitoring
