Why Smart Lighting Detection?

Our project aims to address the growing concern of energy waste in lighting systems. By implementing smart detection technology, we can significantly reduce unnecessary energy consumption while maintaining optimal lighting conditions.

Key Benefits

  • Energy Efficiency

    Smart detection ensures lights are only active when needed, reducing energy waste.

  • Cost Savings

    Lower energy consumption leads to significant reduction in electricity bills.

  • Environmental Impact

    Reduced energy usage means lower carbon emissions and a smaller environmental footprint.

  • User Comfort

    Automated lighting provides a more comfortable and intuitive environment.

Project Goals

Through this project, we aim to demonstrate how artificial intelligence and computer vision can be used to create more sustainable lighting solutions. Our technology not only saves energy but also provides a more intuitive and responsive lighting experience.
